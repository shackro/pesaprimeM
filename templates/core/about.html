About PesaPrime Capital

Empowering smart investments since 2023

Our Mission

To democratize investment opportunities by providing accessible, secure, and profitable investment solutions to everyone, regardless of their financial background or experience level.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading investment platform, transforming how people grow their wealth through innovative technology, expert market analysis, and unparalleled customer service.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of financial experts and technology innovators, PesaPrime Capital was born from a simple observation: traditional investment platforms were either too complex for beginners or too limited for serious investors.

We set out to create a platform that bridges this gap – combining professional-grade investment tools with an intuitive interface that anyone can use. Our team brings together decades of experience in finance, technology, and customer service to deliver an exceptional investment experience.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of investors across Kenya and beyond, helping them achieve their financial goals through smart, secure investments.
